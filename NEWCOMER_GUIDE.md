# Newcomer Guide: How to Use **My Expenses**

Welcome! This guide is for first-time users of the app. It walks you through the main screens and the most important actions so you can start tracking your money quickly.

---

## 1) First launch flow

When you open the app, you‚Äôll go through this sequence:

1. **Login / Register**
   - Create an account or sign in.
2. **Category Onboarding**
   - Choose your starting **income** and **expense** categories.
3. **Main App**
   - You‚Äôll enter the app with 3 tabs at the bottom:

---

## 2) Dashboard (home screen)

The Dashboard is your daily control center.

### What you‚Äôll see
- **This Week (Net)** and **This Month (Net)** summary cards
- **Week Income** and **Week Expense** cards
- A **Recent** list of transactions

### Main actions
- **Add transaction**
  - Tap the floating **Add** button.
  - Enter amount, type (Income/Expense), category, date, and optional description.
  - Tap **Save**.

- **Upload bank statement PDF**
  - Tap **Upload Bank PDF**.
  - Pick a PDF from your device.
  - App attempts to extract transactions and stage them for review.

- **Review staged transactions**
  - Tap **Review staged transactions**.
  - For each row, choose:
    - Transaction **Type** (Income/Expense)
    - Transaction **Category**
  - Tick rows you want to accept, then tap **Confirm**.
  - Confirmed rows are queued/synced into your main transactions.

---

## 3) Top bar tools (Dashboard)

When you‚Äôre on the Dashboard tab, the app bar includes useful shortcuts:

- **Currency selector (left side)**
  - Tap the currency area to switch your display currency.

- **Sync button (cloud icon)**
  - Manually trigger sync for pending offline changes.
  - A small red badge means you still have pending items.

- **Export CSV (download icon)**
  - Export your transactions and save/share the CSV file.

- **Settings (gear icon)**
  - Opens your account and app preferences.

---

## 4) History tab

Use History to inspect and manage all records.

### Filter options
- **Type**: All / Income / Expense
- **Month**: `yyyy-mm`
- **Category**

### Edit & delete
- Tap a transaction‚Äôs **edit** action to update details.
- Swipe a transaction from right to left (or use delete action) to remove it.

### Infinite loading
- Scroll near the bottom to load additional records automatically.

---

## 5) Analytics tab

Use Analytics for quick trends.

- Toggle between:
  - **Weekly** view
  - **Monthly** view
- Bar chart compares:
  - **Income** (green)
  - **Expense** (red)

If no transactions exist yet, the chart shows a ‚ÄúNo data yet.‚Äù state.

## 6) Settings page

Open via gear icon in Dashboard.

### Profile & account
- Change display name
- Change password
- Logout

### Preferences
- Change currency
- Edit income and expense categories

  - Add your own custom categories
- Toggle dark mode
- Toggle notifications

### Information panels
- **Privacy**: what data is stored and why
- **About My Expenses**: app capabilities and architecture overview

## 7) Recommended beginner routine

If you‚Äôre new, follow this simple routine:

1. Set categories and currency in **Settings**.
2. Add 3‚Äì5 transactions manually from **Dashboard**.
3. Go to **History** and test filters.
4. Check **Analytics** weekly/monthly trend toggle.
5. Try **Export CSV** for backup/reporting.
6. (Optional) Upload a bank PDF and review staged records.

---

## 8) Common tips

- If you‚Äôre offline, changes can be queued and synced later.
- Keep your categories clean; it improves filtering and analysis.
- Review staged PDF transactions carefully before confirming.
- Export CSV regularly if you want an external backup.

---

## 9) Quick glossary

- **Net**: Income minus Expense
- **Staged transaction**: Extracted transaction waiting for your confirmation
- **Pending sync**: Local changes waiting to upload when network/API is available
